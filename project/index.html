This is Dark³, a new way of looking at cyber security.

I was contracted by Brick Design for this one, where I worked with Brick's John Hanusek to build out their UI. 

Completely built in ReactJS and destined to be distributed through Electron as a desktop application, this was a fun one. React was perfect for the task, allowing us to break the UI into distinct components and use those to lay out our screens.
